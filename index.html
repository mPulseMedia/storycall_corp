Exchange messages with anyone—by phone.

StoryCall turns messages from Instagram, text, and other apps into a scheduled phone call for someone who doesn’t use social media—then sends their voice reply back to the thread.

Opt-out anytime: the phone recipient can stop future calls by texting STOP.
A common use case is helping grandchildren and grandparents stay in touch—without changing anyone’s habits.

Try the app demo See how it works Get help

How it works

  1. You send a message (IG / SMS / apps).
  2. StoryCall schedules a phone call and plays it.
  3. The phone user replies by speaking.
  4. The reply returns as audio (optionally transcript).
Simple for them. Familiar for you. Helpful for everyone.

  • Incoming messages: text and/or voice notes you choose to route through StoryCall.

  • Recipient replies: recorded voice replies, and optional transcripts if enabled.

  • Basic logs: timestamps, delivery status, call duration, and error reports.
