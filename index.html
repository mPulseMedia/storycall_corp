FAQ

Does it work with her landline? ›

Yes. StoryCall is designed to work well on old landlines and smart phones.

Does anyone install anything? ›

No. She receives a normal phone call, listens, and replies after the beep.

You use SMS and attach a voice recording —  both native smartphone functionality.

What if she doesn't answer? ›

If she doesn't answer, StoryCall will try again later.

Or she can say, “call back in an hour” or “call back tomorrow” or other calling instructions.

How do I stop? ›

You can opt out anytime by texting STOP.
