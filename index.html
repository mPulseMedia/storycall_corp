Messages in. Phone calls out.

StoryCall relays audio messages from Instagram, text, and other apps into a scheduled telephone call for someone who doesn’t use social media—then sends their voice reply back to the thread.

Try the app demo See how it works

How it works

  1. Someone sends a message (IG / SMS / apps).
  2. StoryCall schedules a phone call and plays it.
  3. The phone user replies by speaking.
  4. The reply goes back as audio (optionally transcript).
Example: grandchild ↔ grandparent, without changing habits.

Who it’s for

  • Families connecting across generations.
  • People who prefer landlines or simple calls.
  • Care teams sharing updates by voice.

FAQ

  • Does the phone user install anything? No. It’s just a call.
  • Landline-friendly? Yes.
  • Only Instagram? No—also texts and other apps.
