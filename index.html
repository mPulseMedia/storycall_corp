FAQ

Is this landline-friendly? ›

Yes. StoryCall is designed to work well on landlines and simple phones.

Does the landline user install anything? ›

No. They receive a normal phone call, listen, and reply by speaking.

How do you stop? ›

Opt out anytime by texting STOP.

Where can I see it in action? ›

Open the app experience at app.storycall.org.
