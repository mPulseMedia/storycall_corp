Stay close—without asking Grandma to “get the app.”

StoryCall turns messages from Instagram, text, and other apps into a scheduled phone call for someone who doesn’t use social media—then sends their voice reply back to the thread.

Opt-out anytime: the phone recipient can stop future calls by texting STOP.

Try the app demo See how it works Get help

How it works

  1. You send a message (IG / SMS / apps).
  2. StoryCall schedules a phone call and plays it.
  3. Your grandparent replies by speaking.
  4. The reply returns as audio (optionally transcript).
Simple for them. Familiar for you.

Need help?

Send a help request and it will be emailed to Paul at paul@storycall.org.
Tip: If the phone recipient texts STOP, they won’t receive future StoryCall calls.

FAQ

Does the phone user install anything? ›

No. They receive a normal phone call, listen, and reply by speaking after the prompt.

Is it landline-friendly? ›

Yes. StoryCall is designed to work well on landlines and simple phones.

How do people stop? ›

The phone recipient can opt out anytime by texting STOP.
